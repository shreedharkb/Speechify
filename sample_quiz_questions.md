# Sample Quiz Questions for Speechify

## Science Questions (5-10 points each)

### Biology
1. **What is photosynthesis?** (10 pts)
   - Teacher Answer: "Photosynthesis is the process by which plants use sunlight to convert carbon dioxide and water into glucose and oxygen"
   - Student variations that should score well:
     - "Plants make food using sunlight" (70%+ - 7.5 pts)
     - "Converting sunlight to energy in plants" (60%+ - 5 pts)

2. **What is the function of mitochondria?** (5 pts)
   - Teacher Answer: "Mitochondria are the powerhouse of the cell that produce energy"
   - Student variations:
     - "They make energy for cells" (85%+ - 5 pts)
     - "Cell's power generator" (70%+ - 3.75 pts)

3. **What is DNA?** (6 pts)
   - Teacher Answer: "DNA is deoxyribonucleic acid, the genetic material that carries hereditary information"
   - Student variations:
     - "Genetic material that stores our traits" (85%+ - 6 pts)
     - "Blueprint of life" (60%+ - 3 pts)

### Chemistry
4. **What is H2O?** (2 pts)
   - Teacher Answer: "Water"
   - Student variations:
     - "H2O" (100% - 2 pts)
     - "Dihydrogen monoxide" (100% - 2 pts)

5. **What happens during oxidation?** (10 pts)
   - Teacher Answer: "Oxidation is the loss of electrons by a molecule, atom, or ion"
   - Student variations:
     - "Losing electrons" (70%+ - 7.5 pts)
     - "Chemical reaction with oxygen" (60%+ - 5 pts)

### Physics
6. **What is gravity?** (5 pts)
   - Teacher Answer: "Gravity is the force that attracts objects with mass toward each other"
   - Student variations:
     - "Force pulling things down" (70%+ - 3.75 pts)
     - "Attraction between masses" (85%+ - 5 pts)

7. **What is the speed of light?** (3 pts)
   - Teacher Answer: "300,000 kilometers per second"
   - Student variations:
     - "3 × 10^8 m/s" (100% - 3 pts)
     - "Very fast, about 300000 km/s" (85%+ - 3 pts)

---

## How These Questions Will Be Graded:

### Grading Tiers:
- **85-100% similarity** → Full Credit (100%)
- **70-84% similarity** → High Partial (75%)
- **60-69% similarity** → Medium Partial (50%)
- **50-59% similarity** → Low Partial (25%)
- **Below 50%** → No Credit (0%)

### Smart Rounding:
- Questions with **1-3 points** → Rounded to nearest **0.5**
- Questions with **4+ points** → Rounded to nearest **0.25**

### Examples:
- **10-point question** at 78% similarity → **7.50 points**
- **5-point question** at 65% similarity → **2.50 points**
- **2-point question** at 72% similarity → **1.50 points**
- **1-point question** at 55% similarity → **0.50 points**
