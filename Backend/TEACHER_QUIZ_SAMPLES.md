## üí° TIPS FOR CREATING GOOD QUIZ QUESTIONS

1. **Be Specific**: "What is the capital of France?" is better than "Tell me about France"
2. **Single Answer**: Questions should have one clear correct answer
3. **Context Matters**: Include enough context in the question
4. **Avoid Ambiguity**: "What year did WWII end?" is better than "When did the war end?"
5. **Test Variations**: Try different student answer formats to test the AI
